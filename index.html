What We Do

What is STEM Racing?

STEM Racing is a competitive engineering program where student teams design, build, and race human-powered vehicles. It combines real-world engineering, manufacturing, and project management with the thrill of motorsport.

Our team competes in the F24 category — single-seat, pedal-powered cars built entirely by students. Every part is designed, fabricated, and tested by our team at Northern Secondary School.

Competitions test not just speed, but engineering design, technical presentations, and teamwork — making it one of the most complete STEM experiences available to high school students.
