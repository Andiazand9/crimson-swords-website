Who We Are

We are the Crimson Swords Racing Team, a group of passionate students from Northern Secondary School driven by our love for motorsports and innovation. Our mission is to make STEM accessible for everyone, whilst building a community of learners and problem-solvers.

Our Journey

Every milestone that brought us here.

Fall 2025

The Team Forms

A group of Northern Secondary students come together with one goal, to build, race, and innovate. The Crimson Swords are born.

Late Fall 2025

Workshop Begins

Working and manufactuaring begins, measuring, designing, and building. Precision engineering becomes the team's identity.

Late Fall 2025

Practice & Testing

Hours of practice runs on the track, refining the car's setup, strategy before regional quilfiers.

Late Fall 2025

Canadian Regional Qualifiers

Our first major competition. The team takes to the track at the Canadian STEM Racing Regional Qualifiers, and makes their mark.

Spring 2025

Best Identity & Branding Award 🏆

Recognised for outstanding team branding and presentation. Our identity, the Crimson Swords, earns its first official award.

Early Winter 2026

Practice & Testing

With Canadian Nationals coming up, Crimson Swords is back at it, with hours spent refinig and designg and car worthy of such competion.

2026 →

The Road Ahead

With a stronger team, new sponsors, and lessons learned, we're preparing for our most ambitious season yet. The story continues.
