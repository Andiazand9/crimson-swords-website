Who We Are

We are the Crimson Swords Racing Team, a group of passionate students from Northern Secondary School, driven by a love for motorsports and innovation. Our mission is to make STEM accessible and exciting for everyone, while building a community of hands-on learners and problem-solvers. Inspired by our school’s legacy, we strive for excellence on and off the track, empowering the next generation of STEM leaders.
